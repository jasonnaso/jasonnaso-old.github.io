Welcome to Ready Aim Fire! Here's how it works. Push the start game or space bar and the gun site will move horizontally. When the site is in a position you like, push the Set Aim button or space bar and the site will then move vertically. Press the Fire Button or space bar when the site is over one of the three targets. If you hit all three you win!!!
Good Luck!
